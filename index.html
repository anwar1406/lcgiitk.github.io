LCG-IITK Network

About the Project

Welcome to the LCG-IITK Network, a pioneering initiative at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur focused on developing and deploying a network of low-cost GNSS stations. Our goal is to advance geodesy research and provide valuable data for a wide range of scientific applications.
